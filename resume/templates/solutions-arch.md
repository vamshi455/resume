# Solutions Architect Resume Template

## Usage
Tailor `resume/master.md` for Solutions Architect roles by emphasizing:
- End-to-end platform architecture (Azure, AWS, Snowflake)
- Reference architectures, design patterns, medallion architecture
- Cross-cloud integration (Azure + AWS)
- Responsible AI architecture, governance frameworks
- Domain expertise across energy trading, upstream, downstream

## Sections to Emphasize
- Full professional summary
- Architecture decisions across all projects
- Multi-environment deployments (Dev/QA/Prod)
- CI/CD, DevOps, Infrastructure patterns

## Sections to De-emphasize
- Hands-on coding details (PySpark notebooks, stored procedures)
- Individual tool experience years
